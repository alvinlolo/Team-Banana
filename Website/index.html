The game changer

Old media is losing its grip on today’s population, and with it, people are becoming less informed. We want to change that by making video a provocative social experience.

Hot Take feels as immediately rewarding as taking a selfie or swiping left and right through Tinder, but is still informative enought to provide the depth and intrigue that news is able to offer.

Entirely mobile first design, so you can consume the news on the way home or work. Watch and participate in news whenever you want and wherever you are.
A new way to consume news. A fast way to know the world. A convenient way to communicate with others.
Engrossing experience, that makes news fun. Share your ideas with others, founding who has the similar idea with you, like it or commend it.
